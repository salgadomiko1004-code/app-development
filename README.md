# Hairspray (2007)

## Quick Info
- **Title:** Hairspray  
- **Format:** Musical Film  
- **Year:** 2007  
- **Director:** Adam Shankman  
- **Music:** Marc Shaiman  
- **Genre:** Musical, Comedy, Family, Social Satire  

---

## Synopsis
Tracy Turnblad, a cheerful and ambitious teenager, dreams of dancing on the local TV show *The Corny Collins Show*. When she earns a spot on the show, she uses her fame to fight for racial integration in 1960s Baltimore. With upbeat music and lively dance numbers, *Hairspray* promotes messages of equality, confidence, and acceptance.
